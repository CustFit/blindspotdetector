# Customer Blind Spot Detector | Customers Fit

**For CEOs and founders of growing operational businesses — find where customer perception is quietly costing you margin, preference, and growth.**

Rate your business from 1–5 in each area (1 = Complete blind spot, 5 = Crystal clear understanding). The lowest scores are where clients are most likely drifting.

---

## PURCHASE TRIGGERS
**What actually prompts your clients to call you**

- Do you know the specific moment or pain point that causes a client to reach out — rather than wait?
- Can you identify what finally tips a prospect from considering you to committing?
- Do you understand why clients chose you this time — versus the last time they had a similar need?

**Quick Win:** Call three recent clients and ask what specifically made them reach out when they did — not why they chose you, but why they called when they did.

---

## VALUE PERCEPTION
**What clients actually value vs. what you think they value**

- Can you rank the top three things your best clients say they value about working with you?
- Do you know which parts of your service clients would notice immediately if they disappeared?
- Have you identified which parts of your delivery clients don't value — or don't even notice?

**Quick Win:** Ask your three best clients to tell you in their own words what they'd lose if they stopped working with you. The answer will surprise you.

---

## FRICTION POINTS
**Where clients quietly disengage**

- Do you know at which point in a client relationship satisfaction starts to quietly erode?
- Can you identify where your delivery process creates confusion or unmet expectations?
- Do you know what clients wish you did differently — that they've never told you directly?

**Quick Win:** Map your client journey from contract signed to renewal. Mark every handoff. Those handoffs are where perception breaks down.

---

## LOYALTY DRIVERS
**Why your best clients stay — and others don't**

- Do you know what would make your top three clients seriously consider a competitor?
- Can you identify the moments in your service that most strengthen client trust?
- Do you understand why some satisfied clients expand their relationship and others plateau?

**Quick Win:** Compare your top three expanding accounts to your top three plateauing ones. The difference is usually in one specific moment of the relationship.

---

## WORD-OF-MOUTH CATALYSTS
**Why clients refer you — or don't**

- Do you know exactly what your clients tell others when they recommend you?
- Have you identified which specific experiences trigger a referral — and which ones don't?
- Do you understand why some of your happiest clients have never referred anyone?

**Quick Win:** Ask your last three referral sources what they specifically said about you when they made the introduction. That language is your positioning — use it.
